PlayMake

This was one of my first real projects that I have worked on (besides working on Scratch and other projects in classes I took). This project was created by me and my teammates/friends as our entry into AcademiesHacks, a hackathon that was hosted at the Academies of Loudoun. In this project, we created some custom tracks through music software and then uploaded them to the site, where the user could listen to them. Additionally, to fit in with the hackathon's theme of Wordle, we coded a game mode called "Musicle", where the user would receive a snippet of one of our sound tracks, and they would have to guess which one it is. While we unfortunately didn't win any prizes for our project, we had a fun time and gained some experience with hackathons, which will help prepare us to compete in the future. You can view it here on DevPost or check it out through my other accounts under the "View My Socials" tab.
